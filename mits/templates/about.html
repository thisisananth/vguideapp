    What is Vguide

    Vguide a productivity application which helps you get things done gently.

    Register:

    Register and login and you will be taken to the decide screen.

    Decide:

    Enter the three most important tasks which you want to complete today. For each task you enter the number of pomodoros ( Read Pomodoro Technique ) generally, half an hour blocks you need to complete your task.

    Track:

    Then you can track it by visiting the site again and entering the actual number of pomodoros to complete the task.

    Celebrate:

    You can also view all the tasks you have completed and celebrate on the celebrate screen

    Planned Enhancements

    There are lot of enhancements coming:
    1. Notifications
    2. Auto population of tasks from previous incomplete tasks
    3. Reports

    Hope this virtual guide makes you more productive.
